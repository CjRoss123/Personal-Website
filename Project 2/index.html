About Me

My name is Christopher Ross and I am a college graduate from CUNY New York City College of Technology with a major in Computer Systems Technology and received a cum laude honors distinction. In my high school, Uncommon Collegiate Charter, I earned multiple honor roll awards and received an invitation from the National Honors Society. Aside from school work, I was an active member of multiple clubs such as the Art Club, Guitar Club, Soccer Club, and Dance Club. Outside of school I did a lot of volunteer work, such as cleaning up and building a garden in Rockaway Beach or reading to children with autism in an elementary school. Also, I went to both George Mason University and Long Island University as part of an art design program focused on coding video game projects using the Unity platform.

As a Web Developer, I am a highly creative and dependable individual with a superb work ethic and a passion for web development. I have a broad and deep knowledge of a wide variety of computer languages, as well as the principles and techniques of website construction and maintenance. I am a quick learner and a team player, with excellent communication skills and a strong desire to grow and develop my skills in the field. I am seeking an opportunity to work with a dynamic team of professionals where I can apply my skills and knowledge to contribute to the success of the organization while gaining valuable experience and advancing my career.
